Store Setup

Upload your TCGPlayer inventory once. Get a permanent link and QR code your customers can use anytime.

How it works

  1. Paste your TCGPlayer store URL below.
  2. Optionally, export your inventory CSV from the TCGPlayer Seller Portal under Inventory → Export All Inventory and upload it here. This makes searches instant and avoids scraping. You can skip this and add it later.
  3. You’ll get a permanent store link and a QR code. Print the QR code and put it on your counter.
  4. Customers scan the QR, paste their wantlist, and see exactly what you have in stock — no account needed.

Re-upload your CSV anytime to refresh the inventory. The QR code and link stay the same forever.
